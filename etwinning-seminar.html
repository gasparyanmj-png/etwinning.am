01 — About

What eTwinning is

eTwinning is the community for schools in Europe, connecting teachers and pupils across borders through joint projects, professional development and shared classroom practice. It is run as part of the European Commission's school education platform.

This seminar brings together National Support Organisations and eTwinning ambassadors to align on priorities, share good practice, and plan collaboration for the year ahead — hosted this year by Armenia's National Coordinating body, NCET.

04 — Venue & logistics

Getting there and settling in

Best Resort Aghveran

The seminar is hosted at Best Resort Aghveran Hotel in Arzakan, a mountain village northeast of Yerevan, over the full three days of the programme.

Departure from Yerevan

Participants staying in Yerevan before the seminar depart together from NCET, 37 Burnazyan St., at 09:00 on Friday, arriving at the resort by around 10:15.

Check-in

Registration and room check-in run from 10:15 to 11:00 on Friday, ahead of the opening remarks at 11:00.

Departure home

The closing session and coffee run until 13:00 on Sunday, with departure from the resort immediately after.
